Kleaning Kraken

Transforming shipping vessels into ocean cleanup systems to combat ocean pollution.

The Innovation

The Kleaning Kraken is an advanced underwater collection system featuring dual retractable arms supporting a specialized net structure.

Environmental Impact

50,000

Commercial vessels eligible for installation

8M

Nautical miles covered annually

11M

Metric tons of plastic entering oceans annually

Based on our calculations, if the Kleaning Kraken were installed on just 20% of eligible vessels, it could potentially collect up to 1.2 million metric tons of ocean plastic annually. This represents approximately 11% of the new plastic entering our oceans each year, making a significant impact on ocean pollution reduction.
